The Business
Spotlight

Our way of giving back to the small businesses we believe in.

For small businesses we've built a genuine connection with, we create a dedicated highlight feature completely free, no strings attached. We use what you share with us about your business to build something that showcases who you are and what you offer, giving you a little extra visibility through our platform and audience.

It's our way of lifting others as we grow. Community matters to us, and we want to use what we've built to shine a light on the people around us doing great things.

✦ The spotlight is always free. If you'd like us to create additional branded content or assets for your business beyond the highlight, our standard service rates apply.
